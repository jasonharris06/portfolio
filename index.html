About Me

My name is Jason Harris and I’m an aspiring certified web developer. I’ve enjoyed being the end user for so many different websites and applications for many years; I love now being able to sit in the driver’s seat and imagine new ways to create a powerful and seamless user experience.

I have spent the past 7+ years working and tweaking complex workforce management software for 1500+ users. During that time, I learned there is power in making a complex process easy for the users, not only do they appreciate it, but efficiency saves companies money and keeps the users coming back.

When I’m not coding, I can be found: listening to music, cooking, hiking in the mountains, exercising and enjoying the company of friends and family.
